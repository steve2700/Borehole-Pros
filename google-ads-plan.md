## üéØ Keyword Match Types Strategy

### Use All Three Match Types for Maximum Coverage:

**Exact Match [keyword]**
- **Use for:** High-intent, high-value terms
- **Example:** [borehole drilling johannesburg]
- **Pros:** Most relevant traffic, best conversion rate
- **Cons:** Limited reach
- **Budget allocation:** 30-40%

**Phrase Match "keyword"**
- **Use for:** Medium to high intent searches
- **Example:** "emergency plumber cape town"
- **Pros:** Good balance of reach and relevance
- **Cons:** May include some irrelevant searches
- **Budget allocation:** 40-50%

**Broad Match keyword**
- **Use for:** Discovery and reaching new audiences
- **Example:** plumbing services durban
- **Pros:** Maximum reach, finds new opportunities
- **Cons:** Less relevant traffic (use Smart Bidding!)
- **Budget allocation:** 10-20%
- **IMPORTANT:** Only use with Smart Bidding (Maximize Conversions)

### Match Type Strategy by Campaign:

**Emergency Campaigns:**
- 50% Exact Match (high intent)
- 40% Phrase Match
- 10% Broad Match

**Borehole Campaigns:**
- 40% Exact Match
- 45% Phrase Match
- 15% Broad Match (discover new terms)

**General Plumbing:**
- 30% Exact Match
- 50% Phrase Match
- 20% Broad Match
