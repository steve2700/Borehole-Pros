**Campaign 3 (Plumbing):**

- Why: Cross-sell opportunity, lower urgency

### After 7 Days (Once You Have Data):
- Switch to "Maximize Conversions" (if 15+ calls tracked)
- OR "Target CPA" at R50 per call
- Keep monitoring and adjust

### Bid Adjustments:
- **Mobile devices:** +20% (people call from mobile)
- **Top of search:** +30% (position 1-3 gets most calls)
- **Geographic:** 
  - Sandton/Johannesburg: +15% (high-income area)
  - Pretoria: +10%
  - Other areas: 0% (baseline)
- **Time of day:**
  - 7am-9am: +20% (people discover problems)
  - 6pm-10pm: +15% (after work hours)
  - 10pm-6am: -20% (less likely to call, but keep emergency ads running)

## üìä CONVERSION TRACKING SETUP

### Step 1: Create Phone Call Conversion Action

1. **In Google Ads:** Tools & Settings ‚Üí Measurement ‚Üí Conversions
2. **Click:** + New Conversion Action
3. **Select:** Phone calls
4. **Choose:** "Calls from ads using call extensions"
5. **Settings:**
   - Conversion name: "Phone Call from Ad"
   - Value: R50 (estimated value per call)
   - Count: Every call
   - Call length: 60 seconds minimum (filters out wrong numbers)
   - Conversion window: 30 days
6. **Save**

### Step 2: Add Call Extension to All Campaigns
1. **Go to:** Ads & extensions ‚Üí Extensions
2. **Click:** + New extension ‚Üí Call extension
3. **Enter:** 060 348 8268
4. **Check:** "Use call reporting"
5. **Check:** "Count calls as phone call conversions"
6. **Apply to:** All campaigns

### Step 3: Install Google Ads Conversion Tracking (Optional but Recommended)
Add this code to your website `<head>` section (contact form submissions):

```html
<!-- Google Ads Conversion Tracking -->
<script async src="https://www.googletagmanager.com/gtag/js?id=AW-XXXXXXXXX"></script>
<script>
  window.dataLayer = window.dataLayer || [];
  function gtag(){dataLayer.push(arguments);}
  gtag('js', new Date());
  gtag('config', 'AW-XXXXXXXXX');
</script>

<!-- Fire on form submission -->
<script>
  gtag('event', 'conversion', {'send_to': 'AW-XXXXXXXXX/YYYYYYYYYY'});
</script>
```

*Replace AW-XXXXXXXXX with your actual conversion ID from Google Ads*

## üìû EMERGENCY TROUBLESHOOTING

### "Not Getting Any Calls" Checklist:
1. Is phone number showing in ads? ‚Üí Check call extensions
2. Is call tracking working? ‚Üí Call your own number from ads
3. Are ads actually showing? ‚Üí Search your keywords manually
4. Is your budget too low? ‚Üí Check if budget spent by noon
5. Are keywords too broad? ‚Üí Add more specific phrase match
6. Landing pages broken? ‚Üí Test all links
7. Office hours only? ‚Üí Extend to 24/7 for emergencies

### "Spending Too Fast" Fixes:
1. Lower max CPC by 30% across all campaigns
2. Remove broad match keywords, use phrase/exact only
3. Add more negative keywords
4. Reduce geographic radius from 25km to 15km
5. Pause lowest-performing ad groups

### "Not Showing in Search" Fixes:
1. Increase bids by 20-30%
2. Improve quality score (rewrite ads to match keywords better)
3. Check if location targeting is correct
4. Ensure daily budget isn't exhausted
5. Make sure campaigns are ACTIVE not paused

## üí° PRO TIPS FOR MAXIMUM CALLS

### Make Your Phone Ring More:
1. **Use Call-Only Ads:** Create mobile-only ads that ONLY show phone number (no website click option)
2. **Call Tracking:** Consider using a call tracking service like CallRail to record calls and track quality
3. **Response Time:** Answer EVERY call within 30 seconds - missed calls = wasted ad spend
4. **Voicemail:** Professional voicemail message: "You've reached Borehole Pros emergency service. Leave your number and we'll call back within 15 minutes."
5. **WhatsApp:** Add WhatsApp extension to ads (many SA customers prefer WhatsApp)

### Answer These Questions When Calls Come:
- "How soon can you come?" ‚Üí "Within 2-4 hours for emergencies"
- "How much will it cost?" ‚Üí "We provide free quotes on-site, but typical [service] costs R[range]"
- "Are you available now?" ‚Üí "Yes! We're available 24/7. Where are you located?"
- "Do you service [area]?" ‚Üí "Yes, we cover all of [city] including [their area]"
